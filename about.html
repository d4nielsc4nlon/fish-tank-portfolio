ABOUT

Hi!

Thanks for coming to my website. My name's Daniel, and I'm a designer working across architecture, spatial design, urban analytics, and visual media. I’m interested in how design can operate across scales—from material systems to cities—to produce more just and environmentally responsible futures.

◇◇◇ ~ ~ ~ ◇◇◇

I spent my childhood between South Florida and New Hope, Pennsylvania, and those shifting environments continue to inform how I think about landscape, development, and the uneven ways people experience place.

I'm interested in low-carbon, public-interest design, with an emphasis on advancing more equitable conditions for marginalized, queer, and low-income communities.

::::: * * * :::::

I studied at the University of Pennsylvania and Columbia GSAPP on scholarship, and coming from a low-income background has shaped my commitment to creating access and community for emerging designers.

I currently live in Brooklyn, where I split my time between school, office work, and freelancing.

I’m always open to commissions and collaborations, and I love connecting with other designers, artists, and creatives.

Based in Brooklyn, NY
